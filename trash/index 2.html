BA ID

The digital identity system for citizens and visitors of Buenos Aires City.

Goal

Our goal is to create a digital identity system for citizens and visitors of Buenos Aires City. Creating a digital ID is the current step in our roadmap to provide better and simple services to our citizens.

BA ID is allowing our users to create their accounts and interact with the City Administration in a smarter and simpler way.

Users access their personal dashboard (Mi BA) where they can find their requested services, appointments for health services, job opportunities and social benefits.

We are adding new services periodically.

Open technology

BA ID uses OpenID Connect, aiming at connecting our city with other cities or with our national government.

We request minimum information to create an account that can be edited, erased or downloaded as a dataset.
